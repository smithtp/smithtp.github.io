Latest News - Winter 2025

I was awarded the BES Aquaic Ecology Early Career Researcher Prize for my recent work on aquatic microbial communities! I gave a talk at the meeting about microbes inhabiting tree holes - small puddles of water formed where the roots buttress up against the trunk of beech trees.

Also had a great time talking about our tree hole work at the main BES conference in Edinburgh at the end of the year!
