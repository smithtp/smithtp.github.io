Latest News

2022-12-05: Our paper "Latent functional diversity may accelerate microbial community responses to temperature fluctuations" is out in eLife! We found that changes in temperature can lead to the resuscitation of previously inactive species or strains, a potentially important mechanism for community responses to environmental change.
